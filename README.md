# 📶 WiFi Billionaire

You live in your parents' bedroom. One laptop. One WiFi connection. One plan:
get **obscenely rich on the internet**.

WiFi Billionaire is a free idle / life-sim about grinding your way from $0 to
owning the solar system — one click, side-hustle, and bad decision at a time.
Set your character's focus, buy their gear, steer their luck, run the
occasional heist, and watch their empire grow in a live pixel-art room.

### Install on macOS
1. Open the `.dmg` and drag **WiFi Billionaire** into **Applications**.
2. First launch only: right-click the app → **Open** → **Open**.
3. That's it — it auto-updates from then on.

### Install on Windows
1. Run `WiFiBillionaireSetup.exe`.
2. If SmartScreen warns: **More info** → **Run anyway**.
3. Done — it auto-updates from then on.

> These are free, unsigned apps, so macOS/Windows show a one-time "unknown
> publisher" prompt. It's not a virus — the steps above are the normal way through it.

## 🎮 How to play

You don't control the character directly — you're the brains behind the hustle:

- **Set their focus** — code, make content, trade crypto, study, sleep, touch grass…
- **Buy gear & upgrades** to multiply income, and pick perks as they level up.
- **Smash HUSTLE** for instant cash, and steer the random events that pop up.
- **Go for the money** the legit way… or run scams, jobs, and full-on heists
  (solo or with friends) when you want a faster, riskier road.
- **Come back daily** for an escalating login-streak bonus.
- **Hit a certain net worth** and something huge unlocks — buy the Moon,
  colonize Mars, own the solar system.
- **Rebirth (prestige)** for permanent upgrades, then do it all again — faster.

Climb the **global leaderboard**, add **real friends**, **chat** live, and chase
**31 challenges** topped by a brutal Legendary tier. Your progress autosaves.
